Top 3 Movies According to Lovepreet

My favourite 3 movies

JODI

Jodi is reportedly based on the lives of real-life Punjabi singers Amar Singh Chamkila and his wife Amarjot Kaur, and their musical journey together.

Kali Jotta

Kali Jotta is the story of those mothers,sisters, and daughters who could neither utter their ordeals nor seek help.

Angrej

Conceived as a romantic comedy set in the pre-partitioned Punjab.
